Quests.

What is Quests ?

Quests is a web application that allows users to enhance their productivity by gamifying the journey to achieving their various goals.

How Does It Work?

Users will enter their goals into their proper categories. Based on the category selected, users will either set small, actionable tasks or a daily habit to make it easier to achieve their general goals.

Once these smaller tasks are achieved, users earn points or XP towards a collectible or leveling-up.

Information about the Inspiration

My role as a student in highschool inspired to develop this website. As juniors in highschool, my peers and I have many academic commitments that we must keep track of.

In addition to these commitments, we have extracurricular activities and other personal development goals (physical health, religious obligations, family responsibilities, etc.)

To streamline the process of managing multiple goals of different types, while also gamifying the experience to make it enjoyable, I decided to create Quests.
