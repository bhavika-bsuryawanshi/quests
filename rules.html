Rules.

Quests' Rules

The user has to collect points through completing small, actionable tasks each day.

There are various different areas these goals can be set in.

Users earn 5 points for every step of a major goal completed.

Goal Areas

  • Physical Fitness
  • Mental Health
  • Education/Career
  • Spiritual/Religious
  • Social Relationships
  • Hobbies
  • Community Service

Examples of Goals

  • Mission
    • Goal: "I want to complete my Application Development project by Sunday 10PM."
    • Daily Task: Complete all items on rubric for Content page.
    • Reward: Earn points towards a collectible figure relating to the Mission's category.
  • Side-Quest
    • Goal: "I want to do the laundry before Monday 9AM."
    • Daily Task: Sort clean vs. dirty clothes.
    • Reward: Earn points towards a collectible figure relating to the Mission's category.
  • Habit
    • Goal: "I want to stay active and fit."
    • Daily Task: Go on morning runs for 10 minutes on weekdays.
    • Reward: Earn XP and level up in the specific area of the habit.
