Pomodoro.

What is a Pomodoro ?

Pomodoro is a time-management technique where one can split their available time into work-blocks and break-blocks.

If you'd like to listen to background/white noise while you work, visit MyNoise.

Time Sectioning

  • 20 minutes - Work
  • 5 minutes - Break
    • Repeat x4
  • 15 minutes - Break
